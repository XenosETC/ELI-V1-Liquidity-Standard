# **1. What Is the ELI Standard?**

The **ELI Standard (Eternal Liquidity Instrument)** is a liquidity and collateral architecture designed for immutable, Proof-of-Work blockchains — especially **Ethereum Classic (ETC)**.

Traditional DeFi relies on:

* upgradeable contracts
* treasury-managed liquidity
* governance votes
* emissions
* liquidity migration
* multisig control

These systems are **fragile** and fundamentally incompatible with ETC’s culture of immutability and neutrality.

ELI introduces a new model:

> **Liquidity that is untouchable, yet fully usable.**

This creates the first **hard liquidity standard** in crypto — a liquidity base that behaves like a public good, not a governable asset.

# **3. Core Components of ELI**

The ELI architecture is built on four pillars that together create a zero-governance, immutable liquidity system.

---

## **3.1 PoBL — Proof of Burn Liquidity**

PoBL is the permanent burning of LP tokens.

Burned LP becomes:

* unwithdrawable
* unmigratable
* non-upgradeable
* ungovernable
* irreversible
* secured by PoW

This transforms liquidity into **eternal infrastructure**.

PoBL creates the first **immutable liquidity base** in DeFi.

---

## **3.2 PoBLBS — Immutable Liquidity Balance Sheets**

PoBLBS are on-chain, verifiable “balance sheets” produced from PoBL events.

They track:

* how much ETC is permanently locked
* how much of the paired asset is permanently locked
* the minimum baseline depth
* immutable collateral backing
* long-term reserve strength

PoBLBS form the economic backbone that anyone can independently verify.

---

## **3.3 ZGF — Zero Governance Finance**

ZGF removes governance entirely:

* no DAO votes
* no parameter changes
* no treasury management
* no multisigs
* no liquidity control mechanisms

DeFi becomes **objective**, not political.

ZGF isn’t decentralized governance —
it’s **no governance**.

---

## **3.4 ELI — Eternal Liquidity Instruments**

ELI assets are a **new asset class**:

> **Tokens and protocols backed by immutable, burned liquidity and verifiable PoBLBS.**

These assets:

* strengthen over time
* gain deeper backing from volatility
* form stable collateral
* build trustless financial structures

This is DeFi that grows stronger *with time* — not weaker.
